Lassi

Lassi has been a main part of my life in summers(Besides frooty). And with the heat growing, I think I'm starting to appreciate it a little more than ever before. I hope you'll like it too. The recipe is from Simmi G from allrecipes.com. Btw I modified it... I hate salt in lassi. So, it will be removed completely to guide young souls in the correct direction. And since I believe in equality, all ages will be guided in this direction. But if you want to add salt, just a pinch is enough. Alright, Let's get into it

Ingredients

  • Ice Cubes
  • 1 3/4 cups plain yogurt
  • 1 1/2 cups ice water
  • 6 cubes ice, crushed
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar

Steps

  1. Fill 6 tall glasses with ice cubes.
  2. Place yogurt, ice water, crushed ice, and sugar in a blender; blend until frothy.
  3. Pour over ice cubes in the glasses to serve.
